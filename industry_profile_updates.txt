=== PROJECT PAGES (DATA SHEETS)
For every project
What is the project, how did you make it, why did you make it
Illustrate *MY* involvement, contribution, and process that went into making the project
Avoid includiong other members
Be humble and accurate
Celebrate/acknowledge success or accolades
Don't *sell* the project, show how your skills were important to the *process* of production
Compelling images (50% process, 50% product)
-> Sketches, diagrams, greybox, layout
Title
-> Title and responsibilities (in simple terms)
-> Don't exaggerate
Tools
-> Let reader determine the skill level
Description (brief)
-> Purpose or intention of the project (competition, targetted audience, etc)
-> Don't sell project, sell your skills
-> How was it a success?
Takeaways
-> Skills learned, how it helped as a designer, what does it motivate you to do more of?
-> How are you better after completing?
-> Be positive - avoid negativity towards project or yourself
-> Should be *MORE THAN HALF* of the page
  -> Why did you work on it?
  -> Why is it being shown?
  -> Why is it relevant?
  -> Why should you be hired?

AVOID Unfinished work
