Minibikes

I've had a couple of pocket bikes kicking around in the garage since last year. $100 for both of them.

Both of them aren't running at the moment, but a carb clean, fresh gas and a bit of cursing should do the trick.

Couch Car

A silly idea of mine turned into a goofy project: make a motorized couch

I rebuilt the engine last year and got the cart nearly running. The engine is mounted, the brakes work, steering (nearly) works: the only difficulty is connecting the engine to the diff. The sprocket I got intersects with the rear axle (I measured 0 times and cut once).

When I get back to work, I'll need to fabricate or order a smaller sprocket (which will mess up the gear ratio but whatever), then hook up the steering and throttle cable.
