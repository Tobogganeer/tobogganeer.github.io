Emotions / Mood

Summary

Emotions and Moods influence creativity motivation positively and negatively. Positive emotions/moods help people with decision-making, while negative emotions can encourage setting future goals and self-improvement. Studies show that both positive and negative mood/emotion theories can increase creativity, although positive ones have a stronger effect. Overall, knowing how to implement each theory in practice can help you with your overall being.
